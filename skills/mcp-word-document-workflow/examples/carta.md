# Example: Letter

## Use Case
Draft a formal or semi-formal letter for business or institutional communication.

## Suggested Structure
1. City and date
2. Recipient details
3. Subject
4. Greeting
5. Body (1-3 paragraphs)
6. Closing and signature

## Minimal Tool Flow
1. `word_create_document`
2. `word_add_paragraph`
3. `word_format_text` (optional emphasis)
4. QA
